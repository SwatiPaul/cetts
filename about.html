Our Story

Connecting People & Places
Across West Bengal

Founded in 2018 with a single vehicle and a passion for reliable service, City Express Tourist Transport Service has grown into one of Kolkata's most trusted transport providers. We serve tourists, corporate travellers, families, and institutions across West Bengal.

Our mission is simple: to make every journey stress-free, safe, and affordable. Whether it's a quick airport run at 3 AM or a multi-day outstation tour, we show up — on time, every time.
