FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Simply call us at +91 9433013425 or send a WhatsApp message with your pickup location, destination, date, time and vehicle preference. Our team confirms availability and sends you a quote within minutes.

No advance is required for local city rides. For outstation trips and long bookings, a small token advance helps confirm your booking. We accept UPI, card and cash.

Free cancellation up to 4 hours before pickup. For outstation trips booked with advance payment, cancellation with over 24 hours notice receives full refund. Call us for any special situations.

Our drivers speak Bengali and basic Hindi. For English-speaking or tourist clients, we provide drivers with better communication skills on request — just let us know when booking.

Toll charges, parking fees and state permits are billed at actuals, separate from the base fare. We always give you the full breakup before confirming so there are no surprises at the end.
